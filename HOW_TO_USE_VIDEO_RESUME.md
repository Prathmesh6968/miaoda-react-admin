# How to Use Video Resume Feature

## ğŸ¬ What is Video Resume?

Video Resume is a feature that remembers where you stopped watching an episode, so you can continue from that exact point when you return. No more manual seeking!

## ğŸš€ Quick Start

### Step 1: Sign In
To use the Video Resume feature, you need to be signed in to your account.

**Why?** Progress is saved to your account so you can access it from any device.

### Step 2: Start Watching
1. Browse anime and select an episode
2. Start watching the video
3. Your progress is automatically saved every 10 seconds

### Step 3: Return Anytime
1. Close the browser or navigate away
2. Come back later and click the same episode
3. You'll see a "Resume Watching?" prompt

### Step 4: Choose Your Option
**Option 1: Resume**
- Click "Resume from [time]" to continue where you left off
- Video will start from your last position

**Option 2: Start Over**
- Click "Start from Beginning" to watch from the start
- Your saved progress will be reset

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Does it work for guest users?
**A**: No, you must be signed in to save progress. This ensures your progress is secure and accessible across devices.

### Q: How often is progress saved?
**A**: Every 10 seconds while you're watching. This balances accuracy with performance.

### Q: Can I disable this feature?
**A**: Simply click "Start from Beginning" each time. Your choice is respected.

### Q: What if I watch on multiple devices?
**A**: Progress syncs across all devices where you're signed in with the same account.

### Q: Does it use a lot of data?
**A**: No, progress updates are tiny (just a number). Minimal data usage.

### Q: What happens if my internet disconnects?
**A**: The last saved progress (within 10 seconds) is preserved. Resume from there when reconnected.

### Q: Can I see my watch history?
**A**: Currently, progress is tracked per episode. Full watch history is a planned feature.

### Q: Does it work with all video formats?
**A**: Yes, it works with any video URL. For best results, use the custom video player with direct video files.

### Q: What if the video URL changes?
**A**: Progress is tied to the episode, not the URL. If the episode ID remains the same, progress is preserved.

### Q: Can I manually mark episodes as watched?
**A**: Yes! Use the checkmark button in the episode list to manually mark episodes.
