## Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console (CRITICAL)

### What is Google Search Console?
It's a free tool from Google that helps your website appear in search results.

### How to Submit:

1. **Go to**: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. **Sign in** with your Google account
3. **Add your website**: `https://animestreamhub.com`
4. **Verify ownership** (follow Google's instructions)
5. **Submit your sitemap**: `https://animestreamhub.com/sitemap.xml`

### Expected Timeline:
- **Initial Indexing**: 1-3 days
- **Full Indexing**: 1-2 weeks
- **Ranking Improvements**: 2-6 months

## Step 6: Monitor Your Progress

### Google Search Console (Weekly Check)

Check these metrics:
- **Impressions**: How many times your site appeared in search
- **Clicks**: How many people clicked
- **Average Position**: Your ranking for keywords
- **Coverage**: Pages indexed by Google

### Google Analytics (Weekly Check)

Track:
- **Organic Traffic**: Visitors from search engines
- **Bounce Rate**: Should be under 60%
- **Session Duration**: Higher is better
- **Pages per Session**: Higher is better
